Super Asteroid Blaster is a traditional side-scrolling shooter I put together. It was fun putting the parallax background together and getting different visuals to work. The Title Screen came out nicely, the asteroids will float by in the background of the title screen. The little explosion was my favorite part to put together, consisting of 8 frames. I'm proud of how well the visuals turned out for a short term project like this.

To the left we have some script from Super Asteroid Blaster in the Game Maker Language, an easy to use scripting language for Game Maker. This allowed me to bind the arrow keys to script that would move the player's fighter jet. When an arrow key is pressed, it will call the corresponding method/function. We change the player's sprite to the correct movement sprite and then adjust their position on the x/y-axis.
